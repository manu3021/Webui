Searching the Calendar

The Calendar tab enables you to view the recorded video clips and alarms for the selected camera(s), based on the selected month or day.

To view clips/alarms for the selected month:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Calendar tab. The CALENDAR page appears.
  2. Click the following at the top of the page and select the required details.
    • Month

      • Note:

      Click the at the top of the page to select the previous or the following month.

    • All, or, Clips, or Alarms
    • Select Devices.
    • From the UNIVERSAL LIST, select the required device(s) and then click Confirm.

Based on the selection, the relevant clips and alarms are listed.

To view clips/alarms for the selected day:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Calendar tab. The CALENDAR page appears.
  2. Click the following at the top of the page and select the required details.
    • Day

      • Note:

      Click the at the top of the page to select the previous or the following dates.

    • All, or, Clips, or Alarms
    • Select Devices.
    • From the UNIVERSAL LIST, select the required device(s) and then click Confirm.

Based on the selection, the relevant clips and alarms are listed on the timeline appearing on the left pane.
