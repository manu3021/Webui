Delete

To delete a customer, site, group or device:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the CREATE & EDIT tab. The CREATE & EDIT page appears.
  2. In the left pane, select the customer, site, group or device and then click Delete. A warning message appears. You can delete the following:
    • Customer
    • Site
    • Group
    • Device

    3. After the required customer, site, group or device is selected, a confirmation message appears.

  3. Click Yes.

Note:

All the entities under the parent node is deleted.
