View Reports

The View Reports option enables you to view the following reports:

  • Audit
  • Card
  • Event

To view a report:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Reports tab.
  2. Click View Reports. The REPORTS page appears.
  3. Under Reports List column, click the required report hyperlink. For example, select CARD REPORTS.
  4. Perform the following functions.
