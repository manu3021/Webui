Managing Profile

You can modify the dealer details, your profile settings, and so on, saved in the Profile.

To modify a profile:

  1. In the Profile tab, click My Profile. The PROFILE page appears.
  2. In the Dealer Details tab, you must:
    • Enter the dealer account details.
    • Upload the dealer profile picture.
    • Select the relevant authorized dealer program.
  3. Click Next.
    Or
    Click the My Profile tab.
  4. In the My Profile tab, you must:
    • Enter the customer account details.
    • Upload the customer profile picture.
  5. Click Save
    The profile is modified.

Note:

To modify the old password:

  1. Click Change Password.
  2. Type the OldPassword.
  3. Type the NewPassword.
  4. Click Change Password.
