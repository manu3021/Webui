Clear Acknowledged Alarms

You can clear the acknowledged alarms after taking the necessary action.

To clear an acknowledged alarm:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Alarms tab.
  2. Click the Actions drop-down list at the upper-right corner of the screen and then click Clear.

All the acknowledged alarms are cleared and removed from the list of acknowledged alarms.
