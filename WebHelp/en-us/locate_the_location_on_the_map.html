Locate the Location on the Map

The Maps tab, based on the Zip codes, enables you to locate and view the location of the selected dealer/customer/site, on the map.

In addition, you can zoom in on the map to view the exact location of the selected entities.

To locate the location on the map:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Create & Edit tab. The CREATE AND EDIT page appears.
  2. In the left pane, click the required entities. The location of the selected entity is displayed on the map.
