Overview of MPC

MAXPRO Cloud (MPC) enables you to manage and monitor your video surveillance anytime, anywhere, using the Internet.

Features of MPC:

  • View live or recorded video anywhere
  • Access on your computer, laptop or mobile device
  • Real-time event notification with clip link
  • Automatic latest feature updates
  • Easily manage and view multiple sites from a single login
  • Enable e-mail notification by configuring rules
  • Unified Video and Access Control System

Supported Browsers

Internet Explorer (IE) 10 and later, Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.

Supported Devices

MPC Appliance and NetAXS-123

Supported Firmware

  • For NetAXS-123 panel: 5.2.27 and later
  • For MPC Appliance: 4.0.29P, RevB and later
