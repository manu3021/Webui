Managing Maps

The Maps tab, based on the selected account(s), enables you to locate and view the location of the selected customer/site, on the map.

In addition, you can zoom in on the map to view the exact location of the selected entities.

To view a location on the map:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Maps tab. The MAPS page appears.
  2. From the top left corner of the page, select the account.

    3. All the customer locations for the selected dealer account is highlighted on the map.

To view the doors and cameras on the map:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Maps tab. The MAPS page appears.
  2. From the top left corner of the page, select the account.

    3. All the customer locations for the selected dealer account is highlighted on the map.

  3. In the map, click the site name to view the floor plan of the selected site.

    4. In the floor plan, you can view all the configured doors and cameras.

  4. Click the door to view the following:
    • Door Status: Displays the status of all the associated doors.
    • Info/Actions: Displays the contact details of the site. Also, displays the door lock and unlock actions.
    • Alarms/Events: Displays the alarms and events (last three card events) of the selected door.
  5. Click the camera to view the live video display.
