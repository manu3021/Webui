Acknowledge Alarms

You can acknowledge an alarm to accept that the necessary response action is being taken.

To acknowledge an alarm:

  1. In the MPC Dashboard area, click the Alarms tab.
  2. Select the alarm/alarms you want to acknowledge in the ALARMS screen.
  3. Under Action and Instructions, click Acknowledge.

Note:

To acknowledge multiple alarms, click the Actions drop-down list at the upper-right corner of the screen and select Acknowledge.
